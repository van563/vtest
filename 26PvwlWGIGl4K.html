Pizza for Breakfast!

I decided to start on February 23 with pizza, to pamper my husband. What is not a Breakfast for the defender of the Fatherland?! Once my friend, who lived in Italy for several years, gave me a master class in pizza preparation. I'll share it with you this secret. Of course, we will not compete with the Italians in this case ,but the pizza turns out really delicious, "Italian".

I always use yeast dough purchased in order to save time. But it is better to buy the ingredients specially, if you want the pizza to be delicious. I usually use some kind of meat product, but of good quality, not boiled sausage. It can be bacon, salami, or some kind of smoked meat. Cheese is better to take mozzarella, not the one that is sold in brine for salads,but harder, which is used especially for pizza preparation. If desired and possible, you can add pepper Bulgarian, as we call it, olives, olives, mushrooms (I prefer to use fresh).

So, let's start: roll out the dough into a thin flatbread, put it on a baking sheet, sprinkled with flour. Next, here is one of the chips, spread the dough with a thin layer of tomato paste.Tomatoes for pica almost do not use,because they are watery, and extra dampness pizza does not need.Pasta also gives a rich tomato taste. Next, in an arbitrary order, we stack, for example, sliced bacon, bell peppers, olives. The second feature is that the top should be sprinkled with Provencal herbs (now you can easily find this in any grocery store), and next, sprinkle with MOZZARELLA cheese (this is also a feature), because with another cheese of the Russian type, it will not be the same. There should be a lot of cheese. There may be few other ingredients, but not cheese. Well, and then in the preheated oven, at about 180 degrees 10-15 minutes to bake and the pizza is ready.

Have a nice appetite!

Well, why not bake one for dinner and please your loved ones? Or go with a pizza to your friends on the light? Baking made with your own hands is always tastier!

Please yourself and your loved ones with your culinary skills masterpieces.
